# Advanced Features

Unlock the full potential of Whatsevr with these advanced tools and features.

## EvrMind AI

Whatsevr includes a built-in AI assistant called **EvrMind AI**.

*   **Access**: Navigate to the AI Chat section (usually found in the main navigation or tools menu).
*   **Usage**:
    *   **Ask Anything**: Type your questions or prompts in the text field.
    *   **Quick Prompts**: Use the horizontal scrollable list of hint messages for quick interactions.
    *   **Context**: The AI remembers the conversation context for a natural chat experience.

## Creator Studio & Monetization

For content creators, the **Studio** provides a gamified dashboard to track progress and earnings.

### Dashboard Overview
*   **Earnings**: Track your **Lifetime**, **Monthly**, and **Pending** earnings in real-time.
*   **Levels**: Progress through levels (**Bronze**, **Silver**, **Gold**, **Platinum**) to unlock exclusive features.
    *   **Unlock Requirements**: Features like **Analytics**, **Monetization**, and **Insights** are locked until you meet specific criteria (e.g., 1,000 Followers + 10 Posts).
*   **Motivational Insights**: Receive dynamic tips and insights based on your performance (e.g., "You're in the top 20% this week").

### Gamification & Rewards
*   **Bonuses**: Participate in limited-time challenges (e.g., "Post 10 flicks this week") to earn extra cash.
*   **Streaks**: Maintain daily posting streaks to earn rewards and keep your engagement high.
*   **Badges**: Earn badges for your profile as you hit major milestones.
*   **Seasonal Events**: Special events with unique rewards and challenges.

## Advanced Messaging

The chat feature goes beyond simple text messages.

*   **Attachments**: Share various types of content directly in chat:
    *   **Photos & Videos**: Share media from your gallery.
    *   **Posts**: Share Flicks, Memories, or other Whatsevr content.
    *   **Documents**: Share PDFs and other files.
*   **Community Chat**: Engage in group conversations within communities you've joined.
*   **Shared Content Management**: View a preview of attachments before sending and easily remove them if needed.

## Collections

Organize your favorite content with **Collections**.

*   **Create Folders**: Group saved posts into custom folders (e.g., "Inspiration", "Recipes", "Work").
*   **Save Content**: When viewing a post, use the **Save/Bookmark** option to add it to a specific folder.
*   **Manage**: Rename or delete folders and remove items as your interests change.

## Reporting & Safety

Whatsevr is committed to maintaining a safe and positive environment.
*   **Report Content**: If you encounter inappropriate content, tap the options menu (three dots) on the post and select **Report**.
*   **Report Users**: You can report users who violate community guidelines directly from their profile.
*   **Blocking**: Block users to prevent them from interacting with you or seeing your content.
