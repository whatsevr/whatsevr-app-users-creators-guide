# Account Management

Manage your personal profile, privacy, and settings.

## Your Profile

Your profile is your digital identity on Whatsevr. To access it, tap the **Profile** icon in the navigation bar.

### Profile Features
*   **Profile Picture & Cover**: Customize your look with a unique profile picture and cover video/image.
*   **Bio & Details**: Update your name, username, and "About" section to tell people who you are.
*   **Stats**: View your total **Likes**, **Followers**, and **Connections/Friends**.
*   **Tabs**: Organize your content into tabs like **Media**, **Flicks**, **Videos**, and **Services**.

## Editing Your Profile

1.  Go to your **Profile**.
2.  Tap the **Edit** button (pencil icon) or **Settings** icon.
3.  Update your information as needed.
4.  Save your changes.

### Professional Details
Enhance your profile by adding professional details:
*   **Education**: Add your educational background, including schools, degrees, and dates.
*   **Work Experience**: List your professional experience, including companies, roles, and duration.
*   **Services**: List the services you offer to potential clients or collaborators.
*   **Portfolio**: Showcase your best work through Offers and PDFs.

## Settings

Access the **Settings** menu from your profile page to manage:

### Account & Privacy
*   **Public Portfolio**: Enable and manage your public portfolio to grow your business or creator journey.
*   **Active Login Sessions**: View and manage all devices currently logged into your account. You can log out of specific sessions for security.
*   **Device Permissions**: Manage the permissions the app has on your device (e.g., Camera, Microphone, Location).
*   **Clear Cache**: Free up space on your device by clearing cached images and data. This will not delete your account or posts.

### Notifications
*   **Activity Alerts**: Direct access to your device's notification settings to customize how you receive alerts from Whatsevr.

### Collaboration
*   **Promote With Us**: Interested in advertising or collaboration? Use this option to contact the Whatsevr team directly.

### Account Actions
*   **Add/Switch Account**: Quickly add a new account or switch between existing ones without logging out.
*   **Logout**: Securely sign out of your current session.

## Verification

*   **Legally Verified**: Look for the verification tick next to user names. This indicates the account has been verified.

## Portfolio Mode

If you are a professional or creator, you can enable **Portfolio** features to showcase:
*   **Services**: List the services you offer.
*   **Offers**: Promote special deals.
*   **PDFs**: Share documents like resumes or brochures.
