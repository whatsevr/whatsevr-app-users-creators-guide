# Getting Started with Whatsevr

This guide will help you set up your account and start using Whatsevr.

## Installation

Whatsevr is available on Android and iOS. Download the app from your respective app store.

## Creating an Account & Logging In

Whatsevr uses a streamlined authentication process to get you started quickly.

1.  **Launch the App**: Open Whatsevr on your device.
2.  **Login/Signup**: Tap on the **"Login New Account"** button.
3.  **Authentication**: You will be prompted to sign in using a secure method (e.g., WhatsApp, Phone Number, or Email via our partner service).
4.  **Profile Setup**:
    *   If you are a new user, you will be asked to enter your **Name** (minimum 4 characters).
    *   Tap **Submit** to create your account.
5.  **Welcome**: Once authenticated, you will be directed to the home screen.

### Account Status
*   **Banned Account**: If your account has been banned, you will see a dialog with details and support contact information.
*   **Deactivated Account**: If your account is deactivated, you will be informed upon login.

## Switching Accounts

If you have multiple accounts, you can easily switch between them:
1.  On the login screen, you will see a list of previously logged-in accounts under **"Switch Account"**.
2.  Simply tap on the account you wish to use to log in instantly.
