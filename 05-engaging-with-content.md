# Engaging with Content

Social engagement is at the heart of Whatsevr. Whether you're leaving thoughtful comments, reacting to posts, sharing content with friends, or building your network through follows, these features help you connect with creators and communities that matter to you.

## Commenting on Posts

The commenting system in Whatsevr allows you to engage directly with content creators and join conversations with other community members. You can comment on all types of content including WTV videos, Flicks, Photos, Memories, Offers, and PDFs.

### How to Comment

1. Open any post (WTV, Flick, Photo, Memory, Offer, or PDF)
2. Look for the comment icon at the bottom of the post
3. Tap the comment button to open the comments section
4. Alternatively, tap "View all comments" link to access the full comments dialog
5. In the comment composer, type your comment message
6. Your comment can be any length, but keep it clear and concise
7. Tap the "Post" button to submit your comment
8. You'll see your comment appear immediately in the comments list with your profile picture and name

> **Tip:** Comments help boost content visibilityâ€”the more comments a post receives, the more engagement it gets!

[Screenshot placeholder: Comments interface showing comment composer and existing comments]

### Replying to Comments

Whatsevr supports nested replies, allowing you to respond directly to specific comments and have threaded conversations.

1. Find a comment you want to reply to
2. Tap the "Reply" button on that comment
3. The comment you're replying to will be highlighted at the top of the composer, showing which comment you're responding to
4. Type your reply message in the composer
5. Tap "Post" to submit your reply
6. Your reply will appear as an indented comment under the original comment

To cancel a reply:
- Tap the X icon next to the parent comment indicator at the top of the composer

[Screenshot placeholder: Reply interface with parent comment indicator showing]

### Attaching Images to Comments

You can enhance your comments by attaching images, perfect for sharing visual context or reactions.

1. In the comment composer, look for the image attachment icon (ðŸ“·)
2. Tap the image icon to open your device's photo gallery
3. Select an image you want to attach
4. The image preview will appear in the composer
5. You can still add text before or after the image
6. Images are automatically optimized for quality and file size
7. Tap "Post" to submit your comment with the image attached

[Screenshot placeholder: Comment with image attachment showing preview]

### Tagging Users in Comments

Mention other users in your comments to notify them and make conversations more personal.

1. In the comment composer, type the "@" symbol followed by a username
2. A user suggestion dropdown will appear showing matching usernames
3. Tap the user you want to tag from the suggestions
4. The username will be inserted as a clickable tag in your comment
5. Tagged users will receive a notification about being mentioned
6. Other users can tap the tag to visit the tagged user's profile

> **Note:** Only tag users who are relevant to the conversation. Excessive tagging may be considered spam.

[Screenshot placeholder: User tagging interface with suggestion dropdown]

### Viewing Comments

Browse through comments on any post to see what the community thinks.

1. Open a post and tap the comment icon or "View all comments"
2. The comments dialog opens showing all comments in chronological order
3. Each comment displays:
   - Commenter's profile picture
   - Commenter's name and username
   - Comment text
   - Timestamp (e.g., "2 hours ago")
   - Number of replies (if any)
   - Reaction count
4. Scroll down to load more comments (pagination)
5. Comments with the most engagement appear at the top if sorted by popularity
6. If there are no comments yet, you'll see "Be the first to comment"

## Sharing Content

Sharing is a powerful way to spread content you love and help creators reach new audiences. You can share posts with individual users, communities, or both.

### What Can Be Shared

You can share the following content types:
- WTV videos
- Flicks
- Photos
- Memories
- Offers
- PDFs
- User profiles
- Communities

### Opening the Share Dialog

1. Find content you want to share
2. Look for the share button (usually shown as an arrow or share icon) at the bottom of the post
3. Tap the share button
4. The share dialog opens as a bottom sheet
5. You'll see recipient options and a message composer

[Screenshot placeholder: Share button location on a post]

### Selecting Recipients

The share dialog provides multiple ways to find and select who you want to share with.

**Searched Users:**
- Use the search bar at the top of the dialog
- Type a username or display name
- Results appear as you type
- Tap a user to select them (checkmark appears)

**Recent Chats:**
- Users you've messaged recently appear in this section
- Quick selection for people you communicate with frequently

**Connections:**
- Users you have mutual connections with
- Helps expand your sharing network

**Followers:**
- Users who follow you
- Great for sharing exclusive content with your audience

**Owned Communities:**
- Communities you created
- Share exclusively with your community members

**Joined Communities:**
- Communities you're a member of
- Share content relevant to those communities

**Multiple Selection:**
- You can select multiple recipients at once
- A checkmark appears next to selected recipients
- Toggle the checkmark to deselect

[Screenshot placeholder: Share dialog showing recipient sections and selection]

### Adding a Message

Personalize your share with an optional custom message.

1. In the share dialog, find the message input field at the bottom
2. Tap the message field to focus it
3. Type a custom message (optionalâ€”sharing works without a message)
4. Quick message templates may appear to speed up composition:
   - "Hey, check this out!"
   - "I found this interesting"
   - "You have to see this!"
   - "Thought you'd like this"
5. Tap a template to auto-fill the message field
6. Edit the template text if needed
7. Your message will accompany the shared content

[Screenshot placeholder: Message composer with template suggestions]

### Sending the Share

Complete the sharing process in just a few steps.

1. Ensure you've selected at least one recipient (the Send button is disabled if no recipients are selected)
2. Review your selected recipients and message
3. Tap the "Send" button
4. A confirmation message appears briefly
5. The dialog closes automatically
6. The content is now shared with all selected recipients

### Additional Share Options

Some content types offer additional sharing options.

- **Share as Memory** - Share the content as a temporary story-like item
- **Share Externally** - Share to other apps installed on your device (email, messaging apps, etc.)
- **Share via Email** - Send content directly via email to non-app users
- **Copy Link** - Copy a shareable link to paste elsewhere

## Following and Unfollowing Users

Following is the foundation of building your personalized feed. When you follow users, their content appears in your Home feed and you'll be notified of their new posts.

### Why Follow Users

Following users helps you:
- See their content in your Home feed
- Stay updated on their latest posts automatically
- Build meaningful connections with creators
- Discover more content tailored to your interests
- Support creators you enjoy

### How to Follow a User

There are several places where you can follow a user:

**From Profile:**
1. Visit a user's profile
2. Look for the "Follow" button (usually at the top or next to their name)
3. Tap the "Follow" button
4. The button immediately changes to "Following" state
5. You'll now receive updates from this user

**From Post:**
1. View any post by the user you want to follow
2. Look for the follow button next to the author's name
3. Tap the follow button
4. The button updates to "Following"

**From Comments:**
1. Find a comment by a user you want to follow
2. Tap the follow button on their comment profile
3. Tap "Follow" to follow that user

**From Search:**
1. Search for a user
2. Find them in the search results
3. Tap the "Follow" button on their search result
4. Button changes to "Following"

[Screenshot placeholder: Follow button locations in different contexts]

> **Tip:** Follow creators whose content consistently interests you for a more personalized feed!

### How to Unfollow a User

If you no longer want to see a user's content:

1. Visit their profile or find one of their posts
2. Tap the "Following" button
3. Confirm you want to unfollow (if prompted)
4. The button changes back to "Follow" state
5. Their content will no longer appear in your feed
6. The user is not notified of your unfollow

### Viewing Your Followers

See who is following you and engage with your audience.

1. Navigate to your profile (tap your profile picture or avatar)
2. Look for the "Followers" count or section
3. Tap on it to open your followers list
4. You'll see all users currently following you with:
   - Their profile picture
   - Username and display name
   - Follower badge if they're influential
   - "Follow Back" option if you're not already following them
5. Scroll through your followers list
6. Tap any follower to visit their profile

[Screenshot placeholder: Followers list view]

### Viewing Who You Follow

Track your network and manage who you follow.

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Look for the "Following" count or section
3. Tap to open your following list
4. You'll see all users you currently follow with:
   - Their profile picture
   - Username and display name
   - Quick "Unfollow" option available
5. Scroll through your following list
6. Tap any user to visit their profile
7. Tap "Unfollow" if you want to remove them from your following list

### Follow Persistence

Your follow list is always saved and available across sessions and devices.

- Your follow list syncs automatically across all your devices
- Follows persist even after closing and reopening the app
- You can have unlimited follows (no limit on how many users you can follow)
- Your follow activity may appear in activity feeds

## Engagement Notifications

Stay updated on how others interact with your content and conversations you're involved in.

### Types of Engagement Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:

- **New Reactions** - When someone reacts to your posts

### Managing Notifications

**Enable Push Notifications:**
1. Open app Settings
2. Navigate to Notifications section
3. Enable "Push Notifications"
4. Configure notification types you want to receive

**Customize Notification Preferences:**
1. Open app Settings
2. Go to Notifications or Preferences
3. Choose which notification types you want to receive
4. Mute notifications from specific users if needed

**Check Activity Log:**
1. Tap the Alerts or Activity tab in the app

3. Tap to view details or respond

> **Tip:** Turn on notifications for users you follow closely to stay engaged with them!

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Cannot Post Comment

**Problem:** Your comment won't submit or you get an error.

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connectionâ€”ensure you have stable WiFi or mobile data
2. Verify your comment isn't empty (it must have text or an image)
3. Check if you're blocked by the content creatorâ€”you won't be able to comment on their posts
4. Verify the post still exists (it may have been deleted)
5. Try refreshing the page (pull down to refresh)
6. Log out and back in
7. Clear the app cache in your device settings
8. If issue persists, reinstall the app

### Reaction Not Registering

**Problem:** Your reaction doesn't appear or the count doesn't update.

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
2. Try unreacting and reacting again
3. Force close and reopen the app
4. Check if the content still existsâ€”it may have been deleted or removed
5. Wait a few secondsâ€”the reaction may be processing
6. Try a different reaction type
7. Clear app cache and try again

### Share Not Sending

**Problem:** The share dialog won't send or shows an error.

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you've selected at least one recipient
2. Check your internet connectivity
3. Ensure the content hasn't been deleted
4. Try sharing to fewer recipients at once
5. Close and reopen the share dialog
6. Try sharing to a specific user first (not a community)
7. Restart the app if issue persists

### Follow Button Not Working

**Problem:** You can't follow or unfollow a user.

**Solutions:**
1. Check if you've reached any follow limit (rare, but possible)
2. Verify your internet connection
3. Try unfollowing and following again
4. Navigate away and back to the user's profile
5. Restart the app
6. Clear app cache in your device settings
7. Check if you're blocked by the user

### Comments Not Loading

**Problem:** The comments section is empty or won't load.

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh the comments section
3. Close and reopen the comments dialog
4. Go back and return to the post
5. Restart the app
6. Clear app cache and try again
7. Try viewing a different post's comments to isolate the issue

### Cannot See Who Reacted

**Problem:** Reactions view is empty or won't open.

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you have an internet connection
2. Try tapping the reaction count instead of long-pressing the button
3. Check if the content creator has disabled reaction visibility in their privacy settings
4. Verify the post still exists
5. Restart the app if needed
6. Close and reopen the reactions dialog
