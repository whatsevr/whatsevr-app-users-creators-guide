# Troubleshooting & Support

Need help? We're here for you.

## Common Issues

### Login Problems
*   **OTP Not Received**: Ensure your phone number or email is correct. Check your spam folder for email OTPs.
*   **Account Banned**: If you see an "Account Banned" message, please contact support for assistance.

### App Performance
*   **Slow Loading**: Check your internet connection. Try clearing the app cache or restarting the app.
*   **Crashes**: Ensure you are using the latest version of Whatsevr.

## Contact Support

If you cannot resolve your issue, please contact our support team:

*   **Email**: support@whatsevr.com (Replace with actual support email if known)
*   **In-App**: Go to **Settings > Help & Support**.

## Reporting Content

To report inappropriate content or users:
1.  Tap the **3-dot menu** on the post or profile.
2.  Select **Report**.
3.  Choose the reason for reporting and submit.
