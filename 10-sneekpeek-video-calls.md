## What is Sneekpeek?

Sneekpeek is a random video calling feature that lets you meet new people instantly. Unlike traditional social networks where you connect with people you already know, Sneekpeek pairs you with random users for authentic, spontaneous conversations.

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Random Matching**: Get paired with different people each timeâ€”you never know who you'll meet next
- **Separate Profile**: Your Sneekpeek profile is completely separate from your main Whatsevr account
- **Anonymous Conversations**: Others see only your Sneekpeek profile information, not your main account
- **Instant Matching**: Join the queue and get matched with available users quickly
- **Automatic Re-matching**: After a call ends, you automatically re-join the queue for the next conversation

**Use Cases:**
- Making new friends and expanding your social circle
- Casual conversations with interesting people
- Professional networking in a relaxed environment
- Practice conversations in different languages
- Meeting people with similar interests

## Setting Up Your Sneekpeek Profile

Before you can start video calling, you need to create your Sneekpeek profile. Don't worryâ€”it only takes a few minutes!

### Accessing Sneekpeek

1. Open the Whatsevr app on your device
2. Navigate to the main menu or home screen
3. Look for the **Sneekpeek** option or tab
4. On first access, you'll be automatically redirected to profile creation

### Creating Your Profile

**Step 1: Profile Picture**
1. Tap the camera icon or profile picture area
2. Choose **Take Photo** to capture a new picture or **Choose from Gallery** to use an existing one
3. The app will let you crop your photo into a circle
4. Tap **Save** or **Confirm**
5. Your profile picture is now set (this step is optional, but recommended for better matching)

**Step 2: Title/Nickname**
1. Tap the **Title** or **Nickname** field
2. Enter a name or nickname (3-30 characters)
3. Examples: "Alex", "Tech Enthusiast", "Coffee Lover"
4. Your title will be visible to people you match with

> **Tip**: Choose a friendly, interesting title that reflects your personality or interests!

**Step 3: About/Bio**
1. Tap the **About** or **Bio** field
2. Write a short bio (up to 200 characters) describing yourself
3. Alternatively, tap the **âœ¨ Bolt Icon** for AI-generated bio suggestions
4. Select a suggestion or write your own
5. Your bio helps others understand who you are

> **Note**: Your bio is optional, but it increases your chances of meaningful matches!

**Step 4: Extended Profile (Tap "More" to expand)**

Once you expand the extended profile section, you'll see additional preference fields:

**Gender**
- Select your gender from the available options
- Options: Male, Female, Other
- This helps the matching system find better connections

**Direct Call Availability**
- **All Days**: Available anytime for calls
- **Weekdays Only**: Available Monday through Friday
- **Weekends Only**: Available Saturday and Sunday
- Choose based on your schedule

**Call Type Preferences**
- **Audio & Video**: Accept both audio and video calls
- **Audio Only**: Prefer calls without video
- **Video Only**: Video calls only
- This affects who you match with

**Step 5: Save Your Profile**
1. Tap the **Save**, **Continue**, or **Done** button at the bottom
2. Wait for the profile to upload (you'll see a loading indicator)
3. Success! You're now ready to start matching

> **Important**: Your profile must be complete before you can join the matching queue.

## Joining the Random Call Queue

Ready to start having conversations? Here's how to join the matching queue.

### Starting the Matching Process

1. Tap the **Start Matching** button at the bottom of the home page
2. The button may appear disabled while your profile is being submitted
3. Confirm any dialogs that appear
4. You'll be entered into the matching queue

> **Note**: The button is disabled if your profile hasn't finished uploading. Wait a moment and try again.

### What Happens Next

When you join the queue:
- Your profile is shared with other users in the queue
- The system starts looking for a compatible match
- You'll see the matching screen with animated loading
- The background runs continuous polling every 3 seconds to find matches
- Once a match is found, the video call starts automatically

### Exiting the Queue

If you change your mind:
1. Tap the **X** button on the matching screen
2. Confirm in the dialog that appears
3. You'll return to the home page

## The Matching Process and Waiting Experience

### What You'll See

When you join the queue, you'll see the **Matching Screen**:

- **Black Background**: Creates a calm, focused environment
- **Animated Loading Pulse**: Three expanding circles that pulse and fade
- **Slideshow**: Rotating placeholder images (changes every 2 seconds)
- **Your Profile Picture**: Displayed at the bottom of the screen
- **Exit Button**: Red X icon in the top-right corner

### Waiting for a Match

- The matching process typically takes a few seconds to a minute, depending on how many users are available
- The animated loading continues while the system searches for the best match
- Be patientâ€”peak hours (evenings and weekends) usually have faster matching

### Background Process

While you wait:
- The system receives real-time updates about available users
- Polling happens every 3 seconds to check for matches
- Your profile remains visible to other users in the queue
- Connection quality is maintained throughout

### Typical Wait Times

- **Peak Hours** (evenings, weekends): 5-15 seconds average
- **Off-Peak Hours** (mid-day, weekdays): 15-60 seconds
- **Very Quiet Times**: May take longer or result in no match

> **Tip**: Use peak hours when you want faster matching!

## Video Call Interface and Controls

Once you're matched, the video call starts automatically. Let's break down what you'll see.

### Call Screen Layout

**Split-Screen Format:**
- **Top Half**: Remote participant's video feed
- **Bottom Half**: Your video feed (or profile picture if camera is off)

When either person has their camera off, you'll see their profile picture with their name instead of video.

### Call Information (Top Bar)

- **Call Duration**: Shows elapsed time (e.g., "00:45" for 45 seconds)
- **Participant Count**: Shows how many people are in the call (typically 2)

### Call Controls (Auto-Hiding)

Tap anywhere on the screen to reveal the control bar. It will auto-hide after 3 seconds of inactivity.

**Control Bar** (rounded white container with 4 buttons):
1. **Microphone Button** (left):
   - Tap to mute/unmute your audio
   - Icon shows speaker with X when muted

2. **Camera Button** (second from left):
   - Tap to turn video on/off
   - Icon shows camera with X when off
   - Your profile picture shows when camera is off

3. **Flip Camera Button** (third from left):
   - Tap to switch between front and back camera
   - Icon shows camera with rotation arrows

4. **Leave Call Button** (right - red):
   - Tap to end the call
   - Confirmation may appear

### Visual Indicators

- **Speaking Border**: A subtle border or highlight appears around the person who's speaking
- **Connection Quality**: May show a small indicator if connection is unstable
- **Video Fit**: Video fills the screen while maintaining aspect ratio

> **Tip**: Keep your phone steady and face the camera for the best experience!

## Ending Calls and Automatic Re-matching

One of Sneekpeek's best features is the seamless re-matching experience.

### How to End a Call

- Tap the **Leave Call** button (red button in the control bar)

4. The system automatically rejoins you to the queue

### When the Remote User Leaves

- If the other person ends the call, you'll automatically return to the matching screen
- Same re-matching process occurs
- No action needed on your part

### The Seamless Loop

This cycle continues automatically:
- Call ends â†’ Finding someone... â†’ New match found â†’ Next call starts
- No manual action requiredâ€”it all happens seamlessly!

### Exiting the Matching Loop

If you want to stop:
1. Tap the **X** button on the "Finding someone..." screen

3. Return to the home page

- Verify stable internet connection
- Ensure your availability matches others' availability

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Sneekpeek free to use?

Yes! Random video calling is completely free for all Whatsevr users. No subscription or charges required.

### Can I choose who I match with?

No, the whole point of Sneekpeek is the random matching experience! This creates authentic, unpredictable connections. However, your preferences (availability, call type) help filter compatible matches.

### How long do calls last?

Calls have a time limit:
- **Production Version**: 2 minutes per call
- **Debug/Testing Version**: 10 seconds per call
- After the limit, you automatically return to the matching queue
- You can end any call earlier by tapping Leave

### Can I skip to the next person?

Yes! Tap the **Leave Call** button anytime to end the call and move to the next match. No need to wait for the time limit.

### Will my main Whatsevr friends see my Sneekpeek activity?

No, absolutely not. Sneekpeek is completely separate from your main account. Your friends won't see any Sneekpeek activity, and Sneekpeek activity won't appear on your main profile.

### Can I block someone?

If someone behaves inappropriately, use the reporting system to report them. This alerts platform moderation to review the user's behavior. The platform can take action against violators.

### What if I don't want to use video?

No problem! In your profile settings:
1. Edit your profile
2. Go to extended profile section
3. Change **Call Type Preference** to "Audio Call"
4. Save changes

Now you'll only match with other audio-call users.

### Can I use Sneekpeek on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access Sneekpeek from multiple devices, but only one active matching session can run at a time. If you start matching on one device, you'll need to exit before matching on another device.

### What happens if my internet disconnects during a call?

Your call will end immediately. When you reconnect:
- Return to the matching screen automatically
- Can rejoin the queue and match with someone new
- Your previous call data is lost (normal behavior)

### How does the 5-second cooldown after calls work?

After each call ends:
1. System ends the video connection
2. 5-second cooldown runs in the background (invisible to you)
3. You see "Finding someone..." screen during this time
4. System automatically rejoins you to the matching queue
5. Next match happens seamlessly

It's built-in to prevent issues and ensure quality matches.

### Can I report someone for inappropriate behavior?

Yes! If someone harasses you, shares inappropriate content, or behaves badly:
1. End the call immediately
2. Tap the report/flag icon
3. Select the reason (harassment, abuse, inappropriate content, etc.)
4. Add details about what happened
5. Submit the report

Moderation reviews reports and takes action.

### Why am I not getting matched?

Possible reasons:
- **Too Few Users**: Off-peak hours have fewer available users
- **Restrictive Preferences**: Check if your availability/call type settings are too narrow
- **Connection Issues**: Ensure stable internet
- **Incomplete Profile**: Make sure profile is fully filled out
- **Try Later**: Come back during peak hours (evenings/weekends)

### How do I update my profile?

1. From Sneekpeek home page, tap your profile card or settings
2. Tap **Edit Profile**
3. Make your changes
4. Tap **Save** or **Update**
