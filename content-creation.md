# Content Creation

Express yourself by creating and sharing various types of content on Whatsevr.

## Types of Content

1.  **Photo Posts**: Share high-quality images with your followers. Perfect for capturing moments.
2.  **Flicks**: Create short, engaging vertical videos. Ideal for quick entertainment and viral content.
3.  **Memories**: Share ephemeral moments (stories) that appear on your profile for a limited time.
4.  **Wtv**: Upload longer-form video content for in-depth storytelling.
5.  **Offers**: (For Portfolios) Create and share special offers or deals to promote your services or products.
6.  **PDFs**: (For Portfolios) Share documents, brochures, and portfolios in PDF format.

## How to Post

1.  **Initiate**: Navigate to your **Profile** or the **Home** screen and tap the **Upload/Post** button (usually a `+` icon).
2.  **Select Type**: Choose the type of content you want to create from the menu (e.g., Photo, Flick, Wtv).
3.  **Create/Upload**:
    *   **Photos/Videos**: Select from your gallery or capture new media using the camera.
    *   **PDFs**: Pick a file from your device storage.
4.  **Edit & Customize**:
    *   **Caption**: Add a descriptive caption to tell your story.
    *   **Tags**: Tag relevant users or communities to increase visibility.
    *   **Location**: Add a location tag to let people know where you are.
    *   **Thumbnails**: For videos, select an engaging thumbnail.
5.  **Publish**: Tap **Post** or **Upload** to share your content with the world.

## Managing Content

*   **Edit**: You can edit the details of your posts (like captions and tags) from your profile.
*   **Delete**: Remove content you no longer want to share via the post options menu (three dots icon on the post).
*   **Image Editor**: Use the built-in image editor to crop, adjust, and enhance your photos before posting.

## Updating Your Username

You can change your unique username to something that better represents you.
1.  Go to **Settings** or **Edit Profile**.
2.  Select **Update Username**.
3.  Enter your desired username and check for availability.
4.  Save changes.
