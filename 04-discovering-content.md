# Discovering Content in Whatsevr

## 1. Introduction

Whatsevr offers a sophisticated content discovery experience that allows you to find and explore content tailored to your interests. Whether you're looking for personalized recommendations, specific content types, or trending posts from creators you follow, Whatsevr's discovery features provide multiple ways to engage with content.

The main discovery features include:

- **Home Feed** - Your personalized hub with recommendations based on your activity and follows
- **Explore Page** - Discover new content across multiple categories (videos, photos, offers, memories, and more)
- **Search** - Find specific users, communities, and content across 8 different content types
- **Flicks** - Enjoy short-form vertical videos in a full-screen, TikTok-style experience

These features are accessible through the bottom navigation tabs. Your Home feed and Explore page are just a tap away, allowing you to easily switch between personalized and public content discovery.

## 2. Home Feed - Your Personalized Hub

The Home tab is your personalized content hub, located as the **second tab** in the bottom navigation (next to the Explore tab). This is where Whatsevr shows you content tailored to your interests and activity.

The **"Your Home"** page features **4 main tabs**:

### 2.1 For You Tab

The **For You** tab is your personalized recommendation feed, designed to show you the content that matters most to you based on your interests, follows, and interactions.

#### Memories Section

At the top of the For You feed, you'll find the **Memories section** - a horizontal scrollable collection of stories from the users you follow. Tap any memory to view it in full-screen mode. Memories are temporary, story-like content that typically expires after 24 hours.

[**Screenshot placeholder**: For You feed showing Memories horizontal scroll at the top]

#### Content Layout

Below the Memories section, the For You feed displays your personalized content in a **staggered grid layout**. This mixed-content layout includes:

- WTV videos (long-form video content)
- Flicks (short-form vertical videos)
- Photos and photo posts
- Offers and promotional content
- Other media from creators you follow

The content alternates between normal and inverted layout patterns, creating an aesthetically pleasing visual flow as you scroll through the feed.

[**Screenshot placeholder**: For You feed showing staggered grid layout with mixed content types]

#### How to Use the For You Tab

**Viewing Content:**
1. Tap on any content tile to open and view it in full-screen or detail view
2. From the detail view, you can react, comment, share, or follow the creator

**Refreshing Your Feed:**
1. Pull down on the For You feed with your finger
2. The feed will refresh and load new personalized recommendations
3. Release to return to the normal view

**Scrolling for More Content:**
1. Scroll down to see more recommended content
2. As you reach the bottom of the feed, new content automatically loads
3. The feed supports infinite scrolling, so you can keep exploring endlessly

### 2.2 Communities Tab

The **Communities** tab displays content from the communities you've joined. This is a dedicated space for community-specific posts and discussions.

**How to Use:**
- View posts and announcements from your joined communities
- Tap on any post to view details and engage
- Use the back button or tab switcher to navigate back to other Home tabs

### 2.3 Offers Tab

The **Offers** tab shows promotional content and deals from creators and communities you follow. This is where you can discover special offers, discounts, and promotions.

**How to Use:**
- Browse through promotional content
- Tap on any offer to view full details
- Save offers that interest you for later viewing

### 2.4 Activities Tab

The **Activities** tab has **two nested subtabs**: **History** and **Saved**.

#### History Subtab

The History tab shows your complete activity log, tracking all your interactions with content on Whatsevr. This includes:

- **Views**: Content you've looked at
- **Reactions**: Posts you've reacted to (with emoji reactions like ðŸ‘, â¤ï¸, etc.)

**How to Use the History Tab:**
1. Tap on any activity entry to navigate to the related content
2. Review your recent interactions to keep track of what you've been engaging with
3. Use this history to revisit content you found interesting
4. Pull-to-refresh to see the most recent activities

#### Saved Subtab

The Saved tab shows your **collections** of saved content - posts, videos, photos, and other items you've marked for later viewing.

**How to Use the Saved Tab:**
1. Browse your saved content collection

## 3. Explore Page - Discover New Content

The **Explore** tab is the **first tab** in the bottom navigation and is your gateway to discovering new content and exploring trending posts across Whatsevr.

At the top of the Explore page, you'll find a **search bar** that gives you quick access to the unified search feature (covered in detail in the Search section below).

The Explore page features **6 content tabs**:

### 3.1 Wtv Tab

The **Wtv tab** displays long-form video content from all users on the platform. Videos are shown in a grid layout with video thumbnails.

**How to Use:**
1. Browse through the grid of video thumbnails
2. Tap on any thumbnail to play the video in full-screen
3. From the video player, you can react, comment, and share
4. Pull-to-refresh to see new WTV videos

### 3.2 Explore Tab (Mixed Content)

The **Explore tab** (within the Explore page) shows a staggered grid layout similar to the Home feed but displays **public content** from all users, not just those you follow.

This mixed-content layout includes:
- Videos, photos, offers, and flicks
- Content from trending creators and communities
- Popular posts gaining engagement

[**Screenshot placeholder**: Explore tab showing staggered grid with mixed public content]

**How to Use:**
1. Scroll through the staggered grid to discover diverse content types
2. Tap on any content tile to view it
3. Pull-to-refresh to load fresh content
4. Scroll to the bottom to load more content automatically

> **Tip**: The Explore tab is perfect for discovering new creators and trending content beyond those you already follow.

### 3.3 Offers Tab

The **Offers tab** displays public promotional content and deals from all users and communities on the platform.

**How to Use:**
1. Browse available offers
2. Tap on any offer to see full details
3. Follow creators with offers you like to see their future promotions

### 3.4 Posts Tab (Photos)

The **Posts tab** shows photo posts from all users, displayed in a grid layout of photo thumbnails.

**How to Use:**
1. Browse through the photo grid
2. Tap on any photo to view it in detail
3. View photographer information and engagement options (reactions, comments, shares)

### 3.5 Memories Tab

The **Memories tab** displays temporary story-like content from all users. Memories typically expire after 24 hours.

**How to Use:**
1. Browse available memories from all users
2. Tap on any memory to view it in full-screen
3. Memories automatically disappear after the expiration time

### 3.6 Flicks Tab

The **Flicks tab** shows short-form videos in a grid layout. Tapping any Flick thumbnail opens the immersive full-screen Flicks feed (detailed in the next section).

**How to Use:**
1. Browse the grid of Flicks thumbnails
2. Tap on any thumbnail to enter the full-screen Flicks feed
3. Flicks open in vertical scrolling mode for an immersive experience

## 4. Flicks Feed - Short-Form Video Experience

**Flicks** are short-form vertical videos similar to TikTok or Instagram Reels. The Flicks feed offers a full-screen, immersive experience designed for quick entertainment and discovery.

### Video Player Features

The Flicks video player includes several key features:

**Auto-Play**: Videos automatically start playing when they appear on your screen, with sound on by default.

**Preloading**: The next 2 videos are preloaded in the background, ensuring smooth and uninterrupted scrolling between videos without loading delays.

**Mute/Unmute Toggle**: A speaker icon button in the bottom-right corner allows you to quickly toggle between muted and unmuted audio. This is perfect for watching videos in quiet environments.

**Progress Indicator**: A progress bar at the bottom of the screen shows how far through the current video you are.

[**Screenshot placeholder**: Flicks interface showing video player with mute button and progress bar]

### User Interface Elements

Each Flick displays several interactive elements around the video:

**User Profile Information**
- Profile picture and username in the top-left corner
- Tap to visit the creator's full profile

**Video Title and Description**
- Displayed over the bottom of the video
- Tap to expand and read the full text if it's truncated

**Quick Follow Button**
- Tap to follow the creator directly from the Flicks feed
- Useful for discovering and following new creators

**Reaction Button**
- Shows a reaction count (e.g., ðŸ‘ 134)
- Tap to react to the video with emoji reactions
- Your reaction is visible to others

**Comment Button**
- Displays the comment count
- Tap to view existing comments or add your own comment

**Share Button**
- Shows the share count
- Tap to share the video with others via messages, stories, or external apps

**Related Content Info** (if applicable)
- If the video is related to a community, offer, or other content
- Shows relevant information and navigation links

**Three-Dot Menu**
- Tap for additional actions
- Options include reporting, blocking, or other content management features

[**Screenshot placeholder**: Flicks interface showing all interaction buttons and user elements]

### How to Use the Flicks Feed

**Navigating Between Videos:**
1. Swipe up on the screen to move to the next video
2. Swipe down to go back to the previous video
3. Videos automatically play as they appear on screen

**Interacting with Videos:**
1. Tap the reaction button to quickly react with an emoji
2. Tap the comment button to view or add comments
3. Tap the share button to share the video
4. Long-press the screen to pause the video

**Following Creators:**
1. Tap the follow button to instantly follow the creator
2. View the creator's profile by tapping their username or picture
3. All future videos from this creator will appear in your Home feed

**Controlling Audio:**
1. Tap the speaker icon (mute button) to mute audio
2. Tap again to unmute audio
3. If your device is muted (silent mode), the app respects that setting

> **Tip**: For optimal Flicks experience, use headphones when in quiet environments, and swipe smoothly up/down for the best scrolling experience.

## 5. Search Functionality - Find Anything

The **Search** feature allows you to find specific users, communities, posts, and other content across Whatsevr using 8 different content type tabs.

### How to Access Search

**Method 1 - From Explore Page:**
1. Navigate to the Explore tab (first tab in bottom navigation)
2. Tap the search bar at the top of the page
3. The search interface opens with an animated hint text field

**Method 2 - Using Search Icon:**
1. Look for the search icon in the app navigation
2. Tap it to open the search interface

### Search Interface

The search interface features:
- **Animated Hint Text Field**: A text input field with helpful hint text
- **Minimum Search Requirement**: Your search query must be at least **4 characters** long
- **Real-Time Results**: Results update as you type
- **8 Content Type Tabs**: Switch between different content types to refine your search

### Search Process

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Enter Your Search Query**
   - Tap the search field
   - Type your search term (minimum 4 characters required)
   - Example: "photography", "travel", "recipes", etc.

2. **View Real-Time Results**
   - As you type, results appear automatically
   - Results are organized by content type
   - If your query is less than 4 characters, you'll see a prompt to type more

3. **Switch Between Content Type Tabs**
   - Below the search field, you'll see 8 tabs for different content types
   - Tap any tab to see results for that specific content type
   - The 8 tabs are: **All People**, **Portfolio**, **Communities**, **Wtv**, **Offers**, **Photos**, **Flicks**, and **Pdfs**

4. **View Search Results**
   - Results are displayed in lists or grids depending on content type
   - Each result shows relevant information (name, description, thumbnail, etc.)
   - Scroll down to see more results

5. **Tap on Results**
   - Tap any search result to view full details
   - For users, you'll see their profile
   - For content, you'll see the full post, video, or document
   - For communities, you'll see the community page

## 6. Navigating Between Content Views

### Using Bottom Navigation Tabs

Whatsevr uses bottom navigation tabs for easy switching between main discovery sections:

**Explore Tab** (First Tab)
- Tap to access the Explore page with 6 content type tabs
- Search bar is located at the top of this view

**Home Tab** (Second Tab)
- Tap to access your personalized Home feed
- Contains 4 tabs: For You, Communities, Offers, and Activities

### Switching Between Tabs Within Each Section

**In the Home Section:**
- Tap the For You, Communities, Offers, or Activities tabs at the top
- Swipe left/right to move between adjacent tabs
- Your scroll position is remembered when you return to a tab

**In the Explore Section:**
- Tap any of the 6 content tabs: Wtv, Explore, Offers, Posts, Memories, Flicks
- Swipe left/right to move between adjacent tabs

### Returning to Previous Views

- Use the back button (if viewing detail of a post)
- Tap a different tab to switch sections
- Swipe back to return to the previous screen (if swiping is enabled)

### Deep Linking from External Sources

You can jump directly to specific content from:
- **Notifications**: Tap a notification to open the related post or content
- **Shared Links**: Tap a Whatsevr link to open the specific content
- **Mentions and Tags**: Tap on mentions or tags to navigate to related content

## 8. Understanding Content Recommendations

### How the Recommendation Algorithm Works

Whatsevr's recommendation system learns from your activity to provide increasingly personalized content. The algorithm considers:

- **Your Follows**: Content from creators and communities you follow
- **Your Interactions**: Posts you've reacted to, commented on, or shared
- **Your Interests**: Content types and topics you engage with most
- **Your History**: Your viewing patterns and activity timeline
- **Trending Content**: Popular posts gaining momentum on the platform

### Personalized vs. Public Recommendations

**Home Feed (Personalized):**
- Shows content based specifically on your activity and follows
- Tailored to your interests and preferences
- More relevant and customized as you use the app

**Explore Page (Public):**
- Shows trending and popular content from all users
- Not filtered by your follows or activity
- Great for discovering new creators and trends outside your current interests

### How Engagement Affects Recommendations

The more you engage with content, the better the algorithm understands your preferences:

- **Reactions**: Show the algorithm what content resonates with you
- **Comments**: Indicate strong interest in specific topics
- **Shares**: Signal that you find content valuable enough to share
- **Follows**: Help the algorithm identify creators relevant to you
- **Views**: Indicate interest in certain content types (videos, photos, etc.)
- **Saves**: Show content you want to return to later

### Privacy Considerations

- Your activity is tracked to personalize recommendations
- You have control over your saved content and activity history
- Review your Activity History anytime to see what's being tracked
- Activity tracking can typically be controlled through account settings (check Profile & Account Management documentation)

## 9. Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Content Not Loading

**Problem**: Posts, videos, or content don't appear in feeds.

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
   - Ensure you have stable WiFi or cellular data
   - Move closer to your WiFi router if signal is weak

2. Pull-to-refresh to reload content
   - Swipe down on any feed to manually refresh

3. Clear app cache
   - Go to device Settings â†’ Apps â†’ Whatsevr â†’ Storage â†’ Clear Cache
   - This removes temporary files without losing your data

4. Restart the app
   - Close Whatsevr completely
   - Wait a few seconds
   - Reopen the app

5. Verify you're logged in
   - Ensure you're signed into your Whatsevr account
   - Log out and back in if needed

### Search Not Working

**Problem**: Search results don't appear or search returns no results.

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure search query meets minimum length
   - Your search must be at least 4 characters
   - Example: "photo" (5 chars) âœ“, "abc" (3 chars) âœ—

2. Check internet connection
   - Search requires an active internet connection

3. Try different search terms
   - More specific searches often yield better results
   - Try variations of your search word

4. Switch between content type tabs
   - Try searching in different tabs to find results
   - "All People" tab searches across all content types

5. Restart the app if search is unresponsive
   - Close and reopen Whatsevr

### Flicks Videos Not Playing

**Problem**: Videos in the Flicks feed don't play or buffer frequently.

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
   - Flicks videos require a stable, preferably high-speed connection
   - Switch from cellular to WiFi if possible

2. Verify device is not muted
   - Check if your device is in silent mode
   - If so, toggle the mute switch to hear audio

3. Try the app's mute/unmute button
   - Tap the speaker icon in the bottom-right corner
   - Toggle between muted and unmuted

4. Close and reopen the Flicks feed
   - Navigate away from Flicks
   - Return to the Flicks feed to restart playback

5. Clear app cache
   - Go to device Settings â†’ Apps â†’ Whatsevr â†’ Storage â†’ Clear Cache

6. Check device storage
   - Low storage can affect video playback performance
   - Delete unused apps or files to free up space

### Recommendations Not Personalized

**Problem**: Your For You feed doesn't seem personalized, or you see mostly unrelated content.

**Solutions:**
1. Engage more with content
   - React to posts you like
   - Comment on content that interests you
   - Share posts with others
   - This trains the algorithm to understand your preferences

2. Follow more users and communities
   - Follow creators in your areas of interest
   - Join communities related to your hobbies
   - More follows = more data for personalization

3. Review your Activity History
   - Check if interactions are being tracked
   - Ensure you're actively engaging with content

4. Give the algorithm time
   - Recommendations improve over time as you use the app
   - If you're new to Whatsevr, check back after a week of active use

5. Verify you're viewing the correct tab
   - Ensure you're in the "For You" tab (not the "Explore" tab)
   - Explore shows public content, not personalized content

### Activity History Not Showing

**Problem**: Your Activity History is empty or not updating.

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
   - Activity History requires an active connection to load

2. Pull-to-refresh the Activity History tab
   - Swipe down on the Activity History tab to refresh

3. Verify you've interacted with content
   - Activity History shows your interactions
   - If you haven't engaged with content, history may appear empty

4. Check if activity tracking is enabled
   - Review app settings to ensure activity tracking is enabled
   - (See Profile & Account Management documentation for details)

5. Restart the app
   - Close and reopen Whatsevr to refresh the activity display

### Infinite Scroll Not Loading More Content

**Problem**: When scrolling to the bottom of a feed, no new content loads.

**Solutions:**
1. Scroll to the very bottom of the feed
   - Continue scrolling until you see a loading indicator
   - Don't stop in the middle of the feed

2. Wait a few seconds for content to load
   - Don't immediately scroll further while content is loading
   - Allow time for the server to respond

3. Check internet connection
   - Infinite scroll requires continuous internet connection
   - Ensure you have stable connectivity

4. Pull-to-refresh to reset the feed
   - Swipe down to refresh
   - This resets the infinite scroll and loads fresh content

5. Verify you haven't reached the end of available content
   - Very active feeds may show a "No more content" message
   - Try returning later when new content is available
