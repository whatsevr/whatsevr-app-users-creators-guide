# Core Features

Whatsevr offers a variety of features to keep you connected and entertained.

## Flicks

**Flicks** are short, vertical videos designed for quick entertainment.

*   **Viewing Flicks**: Swipe up or down to browse through an endless feed of videos.
*   **Interacting**:
    *   **Like**: Tap the reaction button to show your appreciation.
    *   **Comment**: Join the conversation by tapping the comment icon.
    *   **Share**: Share your favorite flicks with friends or on other platforms.
    *   **Mute/Unmute**: Tap the volume icon to toggle sound.
*   **Creator Info**: Tap the user's profile picture to view their profile or the **Follow** button to subscribe to their content.
*   **Related Content**: Some flicks may have related content linked, accessible via the info icon.

## Communities

**Communities** are spaces where people with similar interests come together.

*   **Visual Identity**: Communities feature a dynamic **Cover Video** header.
*   **Community Memories**: Just like users, communities can share ephemeral stories (Memories) visible to members.
*   **Content Tabs**: Explore various content types specific to the community:
    *   **Mix Content**: A feed of all community posts.
    *   **Tagged Content**: Posts where the community has been tagged.
    *   **Services & Offers**: Exclusive deals and services offered by the community.
    *   **WTVs**: Long-form videos curated for the group.
*   **Discover**: Browse "Top Communities" to find groups that interest you.
*   **Join**: Tap "Join" to become a member. Some communities are private and require admin approval.
*   **Create a Community**:
    *   Tap **"Create A New Community"**.
    *   Enter a **Community Name** (max 40 characters).
    *   Set a **Status** (e.g., Professional keyword).
    *   Choose privacy settings (Public or Private).
    *   Tap **Create**.

## Chat & Calls

Stay in touch with friends and family through integrated messaging and calling features.

*   **Rich Messaging**:
    *   **Attachments**: Share Photos, Videos, Documents (PDFs), and even other Whatsevr Posts directly in chat.
    *   **Shared Content Preview**: When sharing a post or link, see a preview before you hit send.
    *   **Smart Scroll**: A "Scroll to Bottom" button appears when you are viewing older messages to quickly jump back to the latest conversation.
*   **Message**: Start a chat directly from a user's profile.
*   **Calls**: Voice and video calling options are available to connect with friends.

## Sneekpeek

**Sneekpeek** is a unique feature for discovering and connecting with new people.
*   **Profile**: Create a specialized profile to showcase yourself.
*   **Discover**: Browse through a list of candidates.
*   **Random Call Queue**: Enter a matchmaking queue to be paired with random users for spontaneous conversations.
    *   **Matchmaking**: The system finds a partner for you.
    *   **Video/Voice**: Connect instantly via high-quality video or voice calls.
*   **Connect**: Engage in direct interactions to meet new people.

## AI Chat

Experience the power of AI directly within Whatsevr.
*   **Chat**: Interact with an intelligent AI assistant for queries, help, or just a conversation.

## Alerts

Stay updated with the **Alerts** tab.
*   **Notifications**: Receive real-time updates about likes, comments, follows, and community activities.
*   **History**: Scroll back to view your entire notification history.

## Search

Find what you're looking for with the powerful search tool.
*   **Global Search**: Accessible from the Home or Account page.
*   **Filters**: Refine your search with specific categories:
    *   **Account**: Find users by name or username.
    *   **Portfolio**: Search for professional portfolios.
    *   **Community**: Discover communities by interest.
    *   **Offer**: Find specific deals.
    *   **Media Post**: Search for photos and videos.
    *   **PDF Post**: Find shared documents.

## Explore

Discover new content and creators on the **Explore** page.

*   **Tabs**: Navigate through specialized feeds:
    *   **Wtv**: Long-form videos.
    *   **Explore**: A mix of trending content.
    *   **Offers**: Discover deals and services.
    *   **Posts**: Browse photo posts.
    *   **Memories**: View public stories.
    *   **Flicks**: Watch short videos.

## Sharing

Share content easily with friends and groups.
*   **Search**: Quickly find users to share with by name.
*   **Multi-Select**: Select multiple recipients at once.
*   **Message**: Add a personal message or choose from quick hints (e.g., "Hey, check this out!").
*   **Content**: Share posts, profiles, or external links directly.
